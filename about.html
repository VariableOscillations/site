☀ Variable Oscillations
Auxiliary Research Station




The primary function of the research station is the exploration of auditory energy fields, to gain an understanding of their terrain and the innate knowledge they possess. Such intangible spaces are accessed through the uncovering of esoteric synthesizer technologies and the development of small scale electromechanical structures. The use of these technologies allows for the researchers of the facility to scan through the various composite waves of the surrounding space time, searching for those resonant frequencies that are bound to the energy fields.
