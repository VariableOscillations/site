 Auxiliary Research Station



The primary function of Variable Oscillations is the exploration of auditory energy fields, to gain an understanding of their terrain and the innate knowledge they possess. Such intangible spaces are accessed through the uncovering of esoteric synthesizer technologies and the development of small scale electromechanical structures.

The use of these technologies allows for the researchers of the facility to scan through the various composite waves of the surrounding space time, searching for those resonant frequencies that are bound to the energy fields. The Auxiliary Research Station serves as a means of dispersing the uncovered technologies so that other researchers may utilize them in their own explorations.
