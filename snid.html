SNID

Sinusoidal Numeric Incremental Device

SNID is an experimental patchable drone synth with three main components, a clock divider, sine wave oscillator, and experimental wave multiplier oscillator. The multiplier oscillator is based on phase distortion synthesis and generates slopes which read through a sine wavetable. The slopes are more complicated than standard phase distortion methods and result in unpredictable and chaotic waveforms. More information can be found here.
