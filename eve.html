EVE-01
 
A polyphonic digital synthesizer utilizing Vector Phase Shaping [VPS] Synthesis made with the modular software Reaktor.

The synthesizer's modules are connected in matrices of attenuators, allowing for flexible audio signal and modulation routing.

Specifications
 
Six voice vector phase shaping synthesizer

Per Voice: 2 Oscillators, LFO, Noise, AR Envelope, Sample & Hold, Low / High Pass Filter, and Stereo Positioning

Requirements: Reaktor 6 / Reaktor 6 Player
